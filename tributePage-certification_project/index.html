Sir Lewis Hamilton

Seven-time F1 World Champion.

Here's a time line of Sir Lewis Hamilton's life:

  • 1985 - Born in Stevenage England
  • 1991 - Receives a go-kart for Christmas.
  • 1995 - Youngest driver to win the British cadet karting championship at 10 years old.
  • 2000 - Named European Champion with the maximum points.
  • 2003 - Secured championship in the British Formula Renault Winter series.
  • 2004 - Earned fifth place in the Formula 3 Euro series championship with Manor.
  • 2005 - Earned 15 of 20 round wins in the Euro Series championship with ASM. Featured as number 24 in a list of the Top 50 Drivers of 2005 by Autosport magazine.
  • 2006 - Joined the ART Grand Prix team and secured the GP2 championship.
